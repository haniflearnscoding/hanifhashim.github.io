Department of Economics Banner

Pull-up Banner Design for Academic Promotion

Role Graphic Designer
Client Concordia University - Faculty of Arts and Science
Year 2023

Project Overview

A retractable pull-up banner designed for Concordia University's Department of Economics to promote the program at recruitment events, open houses, and academic fairs.

Challenge

Create a compelling, professional banner that effectively communicates the Economics department's value proposition while adhering to Concordia's strict brand guidelines and working within the vertical 33" x 79" retractable banner format.

Solution

A bold, data-driven design that positions "CONCORDIA" vertically along the left side in Concordia's signature orange, creating strong visual hierarchy. The main message "DEPARTMENT OF ECONOMICS" is set in the university's branded blue, paired with the tagline "Bringing Economics to life." The right side features dynamic data visualization graphics with cyan bar charts and economic indicators, reinforcing the analytical nature of the discipline. Concordia's geometric brand elements—diagonal stripes in maroon, blue, and orange—add movement and energy to the composition.

Design Approach

Brand Compliance

Strict adherence to Concordia's visual identity system, including approved color palette, typography, and logo usage guidelines

Visual Hierarchy

Strategic use of scale, color, and positioning to guide the viewer's eye from brand recognition to department messaging to call-to-action

Data Visualization

Incorporation of chart graphics and numerical data to convey the analytical, evidence-based nature of economics study

Vertical Format Optimization

Design tailored specifically for the tall, narrow pull-up banner format, balancing text readability with visual impact

Complete pull-up banner design showing vertical layout and brand elements

Design Elements

The color palette draws from Concordia's official brand colors: Concordia Orange (Pantone 1585), Concordia Blue (Pantone 2758), accented with Maroon (Pantone 209) and supporting cyan tones for the data graphics. Typography follows university standards with clean sans-serif faces for maximum legibility at various viewing distances.

The data visualization elements were carefully selected to suggest economic analysis without being tied to specific data points, creating a timeless design that won't require updates as figures change. The overlapping geometric shapes create depth and visual interest while maintaining the professional aesthetic expected of academic materials.

Technical Specifications

The banner was designed at 33" x 79" (retractable pull-up banner standard size) with proper bleed and safe zones to ensure critical information remains visible when installed in the retractable base. Files were prepared in CMYK color mode for professional printing, with all brand colors matched to Concordia's official Pantone specifications.

Application Context

This banner serves as a key promotional tool for the Department of Economics at recruitment events, allowing the department to establish strong visual presence in crowded exhibition halls. The design needed to attract prospective students from across the room while communicating professionalism and academic rigor up close.

The inclusion of the website URL (concordia.ca/economics) provides a clear next step for interested students, connecting the physical banner to digital resources.
