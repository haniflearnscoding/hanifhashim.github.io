About

I'm Abdul Hanif Hashim, a creative technologist and interaction designer based in Montréal. Currently studying Computation Arts at Concordia University, I explore the intersection of technology, art, and human experience through interactive installations, generative systems, and creative code.

My work focuses on making invisible data visible, creating responsive environments that react to human presence, and developing systems that bridge the physical and digital worlds. I'm particularly interested in surveillance culture, machine learning as a creative tool, and the ways technology can create meaningful, embodied experiences.

Technical Practice

My practice combines creative coding, machine learning, and physical computing to create interactive installations and media works. I work extensively with Max/MSP for real-time audio-visual systems, Python for computer vision and machine learning applications, and various sensors and hardware platforms for physical interaction.
